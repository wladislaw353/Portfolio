About

I've been developing turnkey websites for more than 4 years. I've been passionate about the website development since the high school. I consider it my calling.

I'm a creative person, it helps me developing interesting and unique websites. I don’t like routine, so I try to automate my work, as well as the company’s work processes. For example, I created an automated e-brief and workflow system with a step-by-step project management system. In addition, I have my builds based on CMS WordPress and Opencart. These technologies help me to work faster.

Sometimes I'm a mentor and I have my own telegram channel t.me/webdev_life, where I share lifehacks for the beginners and publish news from the web world. I have experience in project management and task distribution, for example, in systems such as Bitrix24, Jira, etc.

I have a good understanding of SEO and how to set up the necessary features for advertising, such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, microdata, etc. In addition, I have experience in developing opencart plugins.

I've graduated from higher education institutions with a master's degree in software engineering. In addition, I took various courses with narrow specialization in web development.

The Progressive Web App

One day I got tired of keeping financial records in notes, I didn't find an app which I liked, so MoneyNote was born 😊

The meaning of the app is to maintain a monthly accounting of finances - income and expenses by categories. The app will generate a graph with statistics and you can always see when a financial transaction was made.

MoneyNote is free and compatible with all popular operating systems and also has the web version
